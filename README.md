# End-to-End-Telecom-Customer-Churn-Analysis
From Excel Data Cleaning,SQL Extraction to Power BI Insights

Project Goal : To identify customer churned sectors and present them in a clear and stragetic friendly dashboard
Key Metrics
Churn Rate : 26.54% 
Churners pay higher average monthly charge than overall customer which highlights that high-value customers are the ones leaving.

Average tenure of Churners are around 17 months. This shows a cliff of customers who has 1.5 years of tenure are at risk of churning. 

Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate while 2 years contract stays lowest which indicates that locking customers into longer commitments is the most effective retention method.

The top 3 reasons are competitor and service related, this indicates that the market is  challenging your current devices and price tiers, also affected by the opearations.

Interestingly, the customers using fiber optic internet type  have the highest churn rate despite being the premium service.

45% of churn is categorized as ‘Competitors’ , this confirms that the main thread is coming from external rather than internal dissatisfaction.

Price is one of the lowest categories. Customers are leaving not because it  is expensive , they are just following better offer or feeling-valued with the service.
